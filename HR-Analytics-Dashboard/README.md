# HR Analytics Dashboard

## Overview

Interactive HR Analytics Dashboard built using Power BI to analyze employee attrition, workforce demographics, job satisfaction, overtime impact, and employee trends.

## Key KPIs

- Total Employees: 1,470
- Attrition Count: 237
- Attrition Rate: 16.12%
- Average Age: 36.92
- Average Monthly Income: 6.50K

## Dashboard Features

- Attrition by Department
- Attrition by Job Role
- Attrition by Gender
- Attrition by Marital Status
- Attrition by Overtime
- Attrition by Age Group
- Attrition by Job Satisfaction

## Tools Used

- Power BI
- DAX
- Data Visualization
- Data Modeling

## Key Insights

- Research & Development experienced the highest attrition.
- Employees working overtime showed higher turnover.
- Employees under 35 represented a major share of attrition.
- Job satisfaction levels significantly impacted retention.
